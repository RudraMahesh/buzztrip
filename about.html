About Us

India’s most loved travel partner — from snow-capped mountains to turquoise beaches, we make every trip extraordinary.

Our Story

Buzz Trip started in 2015 with a simple idea: travel should be effortless, authentic, and joyful. What began as a small group of passionate explorers has now grown into a trusted travel network serving thousands of happy travelers across India & worldwide. We believe that every journey tells a story, and our mission is to write beautiful chapters for each wanderer.

Over the years we've curated exclusive honeymoon getaways, adventure treks, corporate retreats, and luxury escapes — all backed by 24/7 on-ground support and transparent pricing.
